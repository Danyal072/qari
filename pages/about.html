About Quran Learn Academy

Quran Learn Academy is an independent entity managed by a small group of dedicated professionals. We welcome everyone to learn the teachings of the Holy Quran and the commandments of Islam, irrespective of their race, colour, or caste. We aim to make every Muslim understand and follow the basic principles of our religion so as to ensure we can become a better Ummah and make this world a better place for everyone to live in.

Our easy-to-understand Quran teaching process is a well-thought-out course with the objective of making our students learn tajweed Quran word by word in a comprehensive way. We give each student equal attention to ensure everyone has a fair chance of learning the Holy Quran, regardless of their learning speed.

At Quran Learn Academy, we understand it can be difficult for Muslims to attend regular Quranic classes in mosques or Islamic centres, especially in countries where Islam is not the majority religion. Keeping these things in mind, we created our online Quranic classes to make it convenient for everyone to attend.

Start your Quran course from the comfort of your home, pick a timetable that suits your routine, and begin your journey. We aim to teach you tajweed Quran at your own pace, whether you are a quick or slow learner.

As mentioned before, Quran Learn Academy is an independent organization which aims to help everyone who has a desire to learn Quran. In addition to this, our objectives also include building moral ethics of our students and teaching them about the Islamic way of life.

With innovative one-on-one sessions from our expert Quran teachers, you will be learning how you can become a better Muslim and make your life exactly according to the commandments of Islam. With fluency in the English language, our teachers make the best use of one-on-one online sessions and teach you at a pace that matches your learning ability.

In the end, Quran Learn Academy aims to make everyone understand the teachings of the Holy Quran through online Quranic sessions, with the comfort of their home, at a time that suits them best.

E-learning is now the need of the time and is being accepted worldwide. Success of online training depends on the proficiency of the teacher to keep his/her student engaged and focused. We hire trained and qualified staff who are experts in their domain and have online teaching experience.
